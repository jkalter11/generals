RULES OVERVIEW
Except for the spy (SPY), a piece can eliminate another piece if it is higher in rank however the spy (SPY) can eliminate all officers from the rank of Sergeant (SGT) up to 5-Star General/General of the Army (GOA) including the flag (FLG). The ONLY PIECE that can eliminate the spy (SPY) is the private (PVT). The flag (FLG) can also the opposing flag (FLG) as long as it takes the aggressive action against the enemy flag (FLG). In other words, the flag (FLG) can eliminate another flag (FLG).

For more information, visit WIKIPEDIA.

30 MOVE RULE
This is basically a rule that states that if there is NO CHALLENGE in 30 moves (both player moves). Then we will compute who is the winner according to the game pieces left. The values for each piece is based on the table above which is based on the values of the tournament rules. But take note that this 30 move rule is not the same as the 30 move rule of the tournament.
