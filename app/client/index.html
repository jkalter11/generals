About Game of the Generals

This is a proudly Filipino-invented game by Sofronio H. Pasola, Jr. in 1970.

The game simulates armies at war trying to outflank and outmaneuver each other. The main objective of the game is to eliminate or capture the opponent's FLAG piece or alternatively, bring your own FLAG piece to the opponent's end of the board. This is easy to play.

All you have to do is remember who's rank is greater than who (eg: a 5 star general (GOA) is greater than a 3 star (LTG) or 4 star general (GEN) but all these generals and ranked pieces are no match to the spy (SPY) yet the spy (SPY) is no match to the unranked private (PVT)).
